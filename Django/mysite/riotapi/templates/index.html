About our Project

Our definition of "carrying"

Carrying isn't just about KDA.

These are just some of the many criteria we considered when determining whether a champion carried:

kill participation, percentage of team damage, percentage of team gold earned.

Using machine learning along with our criteria for carrying, we analyzed 50,000 total matches from each patch (pre and post patch 5.13) over five regions to render the data that you see now.
